<hr> Horizontal Rule tag:

Used to put a horizontal line in the page. It is a self closing tag.

<title> Title tag:

It is basically the title of the website that is shown at the top.

<center> Centered Text element:

It is used to alight a portion of the text horizontally center by enclosing them with the start and end center tag. It is not a self closing tag.
